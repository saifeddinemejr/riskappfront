Operational Risk Management

In today's complex financial environment, effective operational risk management is crucial for banking institutions. This project, part of my final year at Esprit School of Business, involves developing a web application for BH Bank to implement an operational risk map. Utilizing Angular, a leading front-end framework, this application aims to enhance the bank's risk management processes.
